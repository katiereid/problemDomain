About Cat-Mate

Congratulations on deciding to adopt a cat! It's always best to adopt and we are happy you have chosen Cat-Mate to find your next cat. All cats are great but when you adopt a cat that is also your soulmate, it's just magical! We exist to help you find your cat soulmate.

All you have to do is enter in how far you're willing to travel to find your cat bestie and we'll show you a list of local shelters with kitties available for adoption. Then, enter what personality trait you look for most in a cat companion and we'll return you with a great selection of cats ready for new homes. Once you pick a cat, go visit the shelter and bring home your new friend. Happy Cat Soulmate Searching!
